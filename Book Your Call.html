Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect to see results?

Most students notice improvements pretty fast, often within a week or two. But remember, everyone's different. The key is actually to use the techniques we teach. If you do, you should see your practice scores start to climb pretty quickly.

Do I need to allocate extra study time to benefit from this program?

We focus on boosting your score without adding study time. Most programs just give you more to memorize. We teach you how to use what you already know more effectively. It's about working smarter, not harder.

What kind of support do I get with this program?

We've got your back! You'll get all the training materials, of course, plus a 14-day plan to help you hit the ground running. If you have questions, just shoot us an email. We're here to help. 

How much time do I need to spend on this program each day? 

Not much at all! The whole point is to save you time. Most students spend about 15-30 minutes a day on our techniques. It's short, focused practice that makes a big difference.

What exactly happens in the Analysis Session?

During our focused 15-minute call, we'll dig deep into your current study approach to understand what's working and what isn't. I'll analyze your specific situation, help you identify opportunities for score improvement, and show you how the question-reading techniques work. You'll walk away with a plan that lays out a path for maximum score gains.
